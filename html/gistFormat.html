Gist URL Formatting

Format: (optional items are in (), replace properties in {}) Example:
(https://)gist.github.com/{user}/{gistId} https://gist.github.com/pageletdev/c7509ae023632f4fe8bd
(https://)gist.github.com/{user}/{gistId}/{version} gist.github.com/pageletdev/c7509ae023632f4fe8bd/10366dd5167100d00948cd259958309681241813
(https://)gist.githubusercontent.com/{user}/{gistId}/raw/{filename} https://gist.githubusercontent.com/pageletdev/c7509ae023632f4fe8bd/raw/index.html
(https://)gist.githubusercontent.com/{user}/{gistId}/raw/{version}/{filename} gist.githubusercontent.com/pageletdev/c7509ae023632f4fe8bd/raw/10366dd5167100d00948cd259958309681241813/index.html
